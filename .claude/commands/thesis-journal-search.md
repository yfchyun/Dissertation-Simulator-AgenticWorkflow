# Journal Search

Find suitable academic journals for thesis publication.

## Protocol

1. Read thesis topic, methodology, and key findings from SOT
2. Delegate to @publication-strategist:
   - Identify top 10 candidate journals by fit
   - Assess acceptance rates and review timelines
   - Check scope alignment with thesis topic
   - Evaluate impact factors and indexing (SSCI, SCI, SCOPUS, KCI)
3. Display ranked journal list:
   - Journal name, publisher, impact factor
   - Scope fit score
   - Estimated review timeline
   - Submission requirements summary
4. Record journal recommendations in SOT
